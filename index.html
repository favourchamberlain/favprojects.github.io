Aurora Threads Boutique Analysis Using Power BI

Aurora is a high-end fashion boutique specializing in designer clothing, shoes and accessories. The boutique aims to stay ahead in the competitive retail market by leveraging data-driven strategies. The boutique faces challenges in deriving actionable insights from its growing volume of data. These challenges include difficulty in identifying trends across different product categories and inefficiencies in performance tracking. At the end of this project, i was able to identify that accessories was their best selling item and unisex products were being sold more across the three stores. Also when products are being sold with discount, less revenue are being generated.

MediTrack Health Hospital Analysis using Power Bi

MediTrack Global Health is an international healthcare organization modelled after the UK's National Health Service (NHS). It operates hospitals and medical centers across ten countries, providing patient care, medical consultations, diagnostics, and treatment services. The organization prides itself on delivering high-quality, patient-centered care across its facilities, maintaining efficient hospital operations, and best practices to ensure consistent standards of medical service across all locations. Despite having a well-structured data model and several performance metrics calculated using DAX, MediTrack lacks a visual and interactive way to monitor key business indicators and uncover insights at a glance. The Management needs a dashboard that effectively communicates trends, track performance, and supports timely data-driven decisions.
